Nan Chen
MSc

I am currently a PhD Candidate of EEMCS-DMB group at University of Twente and I work on the SoilProS program under the supervision of Doina Bucur. Before joining UT, I was a Machine Learning Engineer at Baidu in China. My research interests include network data science and machine learning. I used to work in the field of brain networks and now I am heading towards ecological networks. I enjoy exploring different kinds of complex systems using network science methods.
